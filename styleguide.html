Color Palette

A uniform palette creates familiarity, consistency and authenticity with our brand. A user can make meaningful connections easier when visual elements work together.

The primary palette strengthens brand recognition by unifying elements through color.

Our primary colors makes content stand out through contrast. These core colors should be used in all designs across You Have Mail.com®.

You Have Mail® Blue: #333366
Light Background Gray: #F7F7F7
White: #FFFFFF
Black: #000000

Secondary palette complements the core color identity.

Our secondary colors support official colors, creating a wider and more flexible range of options. But they should not be recognized as a part of the official You Have Mail® branding.
